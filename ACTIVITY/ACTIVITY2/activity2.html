Sports

Sports are physical activities that involve skill, teamwork, and competition. They help improve fitness, build character, and teach important values such as discipline, perseverance, and respect. Participating in sports can also foster friendships, relieve stress, and encourage a healthy lifestyle. Whether played individually or as part of a team, sports are a fun and rewarding way to stay active and engaged.

Science

Science is the study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis. It helps us understand how things work, from the smallest atoms to the vastness of space. Scientific discoveries have led to advances in medicine, technology, and our daily lives. By asking questions and seeking evidence, science encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. The pursuit of science continues to shape our future and improve the quality of life for people around the world.

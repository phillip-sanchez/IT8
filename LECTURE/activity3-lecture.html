Artist's Statement
Pablo Picasso (1881–1973) was a Spanish painter, sculptor, printmaker, ceramicist, and stage designer, widely regarded as one of the most influential artists of the 20th century. Co-founder of Cubism, Picasso's work is marked by bold colors, abstract forms, and a relentless drive to innovate. His art challenges conventions and explores the depths of human emotion and perception. Picasso believed that "every act of creation is first an act of destruction," and his mission was to push the boundaries of artistic expression, inspiring generations to see the world anew.
